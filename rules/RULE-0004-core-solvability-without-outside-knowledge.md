# RULE-0004 — Core solvability without outside knowledge

## Rule

A case SHALL contain all information required to justify its intended solution within the player-facing materials.

Outside research MAY confirm or enrich a theory, but it MUST NOT be required to solve a critical dependency.

## Rationale

Players should solve the case by investigating the archive, not by searching the internet for missing domain knowledge.

Specialized knowledge can be valuable, but it must be embedded in the case in a natural way.

## Acceptable embedding methods

Required contextual knowledge MAY appear in:

- local newspaper articles
- expert notes
- industry bulletins
- workplace safety documents
- manuals
- brochures
- marginalia
- training documents
- correspondence

## Validation

A validator SHOULD identify all critical dependencies that require domain knowledge.

For each dependency, it SHOULD confirm that an in-archive source provides enough context for a reasonable player to use it.
